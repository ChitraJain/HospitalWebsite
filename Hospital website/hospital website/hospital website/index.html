WELCOME TO HOSPITAL SERVICE



Government Multi Specialty Hospital, Sector 16, Chandigarh earlier known as General Hospital, is the oldest hospital in the city which is well connected by rail, road and air. The hospital is situated on Madhya Marg of the city, just 300 mtrs from the well known Rose Garden, Sector 16, Chandigarh.


This is a patient friendly hospital, known for prompt and efficient services. It caters to the needs of all alike. This 500 bedded hospital has 24 hrs round the clock emergency and indoor facilities manned by specialists.


The institute intends to develop website in order to facilitate an efficient, speedy and transparent information to the public and other agencies using electronic means so that people sitting at home / work places can know about the dynamic / updated facilities in Emergency / CCU / Trauma etc. and type of facilities which the institute is providing on day to day basis. This will ensure better utilization of services of our institute by the people and also important information can be shared by other hospitals and benefits of the same can be utilized in terms of synchronization of Emergency services etc.

Best Medical & Health care Needs to Our Patients

Based upon modern technology and the utilization of the modern approach for the building of any complex, specially a large hospital, the Chief Architect, Chandigarh Administration evolved a new concept based on the modular system with self-contained modules for different functions with vertical and horizontal integration, a central core which will take away the use of unending corridors of the traditional hospital buildings constructed in the past.Hospital building has been constructed with the highest specifications available in the country so that it can be kept hygienically clean and relatively infection free. Crevices, projections and other designs, which can collect dust and cobwebs, have been avoided in the construction.
